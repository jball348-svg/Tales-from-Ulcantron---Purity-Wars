# Quest And Content Seeds

## Status

**Stub.** Reserved for the campaign-level quest backlog: main-line quests, side quests, recurring "civilian witness" beats, and the specific scenes that anchor each act. Story structure lives in `07_story_structure.md`; this doc is the working backlog.

## What This Doc Will Contain

- **Main-line quests** by act. Each entry: name, location, primary participants, intent, win/lose/cost branching, alignment-route variants.
- **Side quests** that exist on both routes with route-specific framing.
- **Side quests** that exist *only* on one route.
- **Civilian witness beats** — short interactions across the campaign that register the cost of the war on people who are not soldiers or politicians. These should not need their own quest log entries; they should be encountered and remembered.
- **Optional content** — bookstores, festivals, markets, ruins, magical phenomena, regional folklore, sub-region exploration that rewards curiosity rather than completion.

## Authoring Rules When Filled In

- Every main-line quest should be readable as a moral test, not just a fetch / kill / talk-to puzzle.
- Side quests should illuminate something about the kingdom that the main quest does not have time for. Avoid "side quests" that are really just ability checks dressed up.
- Civilian witness beats should not always have a system reward. Sometimes the beat is the reward.
- Avoid quest patterns that rely on the player being secretly omniscient. The player only knows what their character would know.
- Cross-reference every quest seed with `04_factions.md` so the political stakes never feel invented for the moment.
