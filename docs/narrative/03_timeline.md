# Timeline

## Status

**Stub.** Reserved for the campaign timeline once the named pre-war, war, and post-war beats are locked. Foundational world rules and the three-act frame are in `02_world_setting.md` and `07_story_structure.md`. Detailed dating decisions should land here, not in the overview docs.

## What This Doc Will Contain

- Calendar / dating system in use across Azzentia (and Ulcantron more broadly if relevant).
- A sparse list of historical anchors *before* the prologue (founding events, the establishment of the Rite of Naming, prior multiclass moments that mattered).
- The pre-war year's notable beats — the doctrinal hardening, the princess's earlier private inflection points, the specific civic controversies that primed the kingdom for fracture.
- The war timeline — month-resolution, not day-resolution. Major flashpoints and battles named in `04_factions.md`.
- The post-war restoration year — the visible inflection beats players see in Act III.

## Authoring Rules When Filled In

- Every dated beat should be testable against the broad outcome rule in `07_story_structure.md`. If something on the timeline would change which side wins the war, it belongs in route-specific notes, not the canon timeline.
- Dates can be relative ("Year of the Rite", "First Spring of the War") rather than numeric until a calendar is committed.
- Avoid inventing prior wars or off-stage cataclysms that would dilute *Purity Wars* as the central event of the kingdom's recent memory.
